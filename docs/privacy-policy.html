Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Zoldieyk Music Bot  ·  Effective: May 2026  ·  Version 1.0

🔒

We do not store your personal data

Zoldieyk Music Bot is a stateless service. All session data is held in memory only and is permanently erased when the bot restarts. No databases. No logs saved to disk. No tracking.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information Zoldieyk Music Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us") accesses, how it is used, and how long it is retained. We are committed to handling any data with transparency and care.

By using the Bot, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Access

To function, the Bot reads the following Discord data during an active session:

Data Why Retention
Discord username Display "Requested by" in Now Playing embed Session only
Message content Parse song search queries and dot commands Not stored
Voice channel ID Join the correct voice channel for playback Session only
Guild (server) ID Manage per-server queues Session only
Song request counts Power .topsongs and .mystats leaderboards Session only

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Real names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Private messages or direct messages
  • Payment or financial information of any kind
  • Messages from channels other than the designated music channel
  • Any data from servers the Bot is not explicitly invited to

Data Retention

All data held by the Bot exists exclusively in memory (RAM) during runtime. This means:

Every queue, play count, history entry, and username is permanently erased when the Bot restarts. Nothing is written to disk, a database, or any external storage. There is nothing to delete — it is already gone.

Third-Party Services

The Bot interacts with the following third-party services to deliver audio:

  • Discord — platform provider. Subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy at discord.com/privacy
  • Lavalink / Serenetia public node — audio streaming backend. Song search queries are passed to this service to resolve audio
  • YouTube — audio source. Search queries may be sent to YouTube's servers via the Lavalink node

We do not control the data practices of these third parties. Please review their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has interacted with the Bot and you have concerns, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at the same URL. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
